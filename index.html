Brief Introduction

I'm a 2nd Year PhD student, working with MYRIADS team of IRISA, under Prof. Guillaume Pierre. My PhD thesis is on efficient cloud application deployment in Edge Cloud/Fog Computing Infrastructures. I'm using container tools like Docker, Kuberneters, LXC etc. to deploy edge applications in the MEC Testbed for my work. My thesis was started in October 2016 and expected to complete by Septermber 2019, and the work is funded by Ministry of Education, France (MESR PhD contract). Apart from doing research, I also involve in teaching activities(Teaching Mission) in ISTIC department of University of Rennes 1.

I am an Indian, and was born and brought in a small village near Silchar town, of Assam state, India.
